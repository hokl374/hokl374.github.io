Singapore Airlines App Challenge 2018

We developed an integrated suite of solutions to tackle two business challenges faced by Singapore Airlines (SIA) in their operations:

  1. Tracking of unconsumed food & beverages:
    • Developed a Food Package Counting System, using a QR code scanner paired with a proposed hardware design, that automatically tracks and analyses food wastage.
    • Estimated Savings for SIA: $4m p.a. in Food Waste.
  2. Tracking movement of catering inventory:
    • Combining computer vision with intuitively designed hardware.
    • Cutlery Counting System: Using cutlery features (e.g. length, width, prongs) to automatically sort and count cutlery that have been washed.
    • Crockery Counting System: Processed image feed from a camera attached to dishwasher, to classify and count crockery.
    • Estimated Savings for SIA: $3.6m p.a. in Manpower Costs.

Our holistic system automates the process of collecting and analysing inventory and F&B wastage data. Designed to be easily integrated into existing workflows and systems.

Estimated ROI: 6 months - 1 year

Maybank Kim Eng Hackfest 2018

Proposed a framework for data-driven investing and portfolio construction, targeted at retail investors.

A two-part solution involving an (1) Alpha Factors Analyzer and (2) Portfolio Optimizer.

  1. Alpha Factors Analyzer:
    • Using common Alpha Factors (e.g. size, value, momentum etc.) to classify stocks, and provide investors with an intuitive visual representation of stock characteristics at a glance.
    • By regressing Alpha Factors against cross-sectional stock returns, the model is able to provide accurate, out-of-sample, expected risk and return forecasts.
  2. Portfolio Optimizer:
    • Provides investors with an intuitive visualization of portfolio characteristics. The tool also uses Mean-Variance Optimization (MVO) to automatically allocate portfolios given user constraints on expected return, risk and sector/factor exposures.
